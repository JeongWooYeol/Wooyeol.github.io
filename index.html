Wooyeol Jeong

I am an M.S. student at the Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence, POSTECH, advised by Prof. Young-joo Suh. My research interests aim to enable human-like interaction through AI by developing natural and immersive human-AI interfaces. In particular, I focus on multimodal interaction, including context-aware agents that integrate multiple modalities to understand user intent and generate appropriate responses. My vision is to contribute to others' lives and make a positive impact on the world.
